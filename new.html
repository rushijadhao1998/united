United Rural - Types of Loans

United Rural Credit Co-Op. Society Ltd. offers a range of loan products designed to meet the diverse financial needs of individuals, farmers, entrepreneurs, and small businesses in rural and semi-urban areas. Whether it's for personal needs, business growth, or agriculture, we provide loans with flexible terms, competitive interest rates, and quick approvals.

  • Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that can be used for a variety of personal needs such as medical emergencies, education, weddings, or travel. No collateral is required, and repayment is done through easy EMIs. Enjoy attractive interest rates and fast processing with United Rural. Loan amounts up to ₹5 lakhs are available based on eligibility.

  • Business Loan

Our business loan helps entrepreneurs and small businesses finance their operations, purchase inventory, or expand. We offer loans with flexible repayment schedules and competitive interest rates. Collateral-free options are available for eligible customers. Loan amounts range from ₹50,000 to ₹10 lakhs.

  • Agricultural Loan

Agricultural loans are designed to help farmers meet seasonal farming expenses like seeds, fertilizers, irrigation, and equipment purchase. Our loans support both crop production and agricultural infrastructure. Benefit from low interest rates and repayment aligned with crop cycles.

  • Housing Loan

Our home loans help you build, buy, or renovate your dream house. Enjoy low interest rates, longer repayment terms (up to 20 years), and doorstep service. Loans are also available for land purchase and construction. Attractive schemes for salaried and self-employed individuals.

  • Emergency Loan

Need urgent funds for a medical emergency or unexpected expense? Our emergency loan offers quick disbursal and flexible repayment. Ideal for short-term needs, with minimal documentation and fast processing. Loan amounts start from ₹10,000.
